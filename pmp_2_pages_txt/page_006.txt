situations. What type of thinking should you encourage among the team members to help them identify root
causes, resolve issues, and make well-informed decisions?
A. Encourage reactive and impulsive thinking to adapt quickly to changes.
B. Foster disciplined, rational, evidence-based thinking with an open mind and objective analysis
O C. Discourage any reflection on thinking processes as it can slow down decision-making.
O D. Promote solely analytical thinking without considering emotional intelligence.
Correct
In complex and ambiguous situations, fostering disciplined, rational, evidence-based thinking with an
open mind and objective analysis is crucial. This approach, known as critical thinking, enables team
members to recognize bias, identify root causes of problems, consider challenging issues, and
evaluate arguments and perspectives effectively. It involves combining rational and logical reasoning
with conceptual imagination and insight, encouraging a comprehensive understanding of the
situation.
21. You are the project manager of a software development project with a diverse project team. How can you
effectively motivate team members to perform at their best?
O A. Rely solely on extrinsic motivation by offering financial bonuses for high performance.
OB. Set strict targets and impose penalties for underperformance to motivate team members.
OC. Understand individual team members' dominant motivators and tailor motivation methods
accordingly.
OD. Utilize a single motivator, such as the project's success, to inspire all team members equally.
Correct
Effectively motivating a diverse project team requires understanding the individual team members'
dominant motivators. Motivation can be intrinsic (related to the work itself) or extrinsic (associated
with external rewards). Tailoring motivation methods based on individual preferences, such as
providing challenging goals for those motivated by achievement or fostering relatedness for team
members motivated by social connections, helps elicit the best individual and project team
performance.

requirements.
