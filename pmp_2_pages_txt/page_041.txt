175. You are a project manager facing a conflict between two key stakeholders who have different opinions on the
project's scope. Both parties trust each other's judgment, and there is a desire to maintain a good
relationship. Which conflict resolution style should you adopt in this situation?
A. Collaborating
OB. Confronting/problem solving
O C. Compromising
OD. Avoiding
Correct
Collaborating involves incorporating multiple views about the conflict and seeking consensus. This
style is effective when there is trust among participants and time to reach an agreement, making it
suitable for resolving conflicts between stakeholders with differing opinions.
