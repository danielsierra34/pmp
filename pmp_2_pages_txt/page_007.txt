24. You are leading a project that involves developing a new innovative product with a high degree of
uncertainty. The project team members have limited experience with similar projects. Which development
approach would be most suitable to manage this project effectively?
OA. Predictive approach.
OB. Hybrid approach.
OC. Adaptive approach.
OD. Iterative approach.
Correct
For a project with a high degree of uncertainty and limited experience with similar projects, an
adaptive approach would be the most suitable development approach. The adaptive approach, also
known as agile, is well-suited for projects where requirements are subject to change and evolve
throughout the project. It allows the project team to adapt to emerging requirements and learn from
the process to deliver the most valuable product.

Correct
