situation?
A. Continue with the current project plan and deliverables without considering the changes in business
objectives.

C. Immediately adapt the project plan and deliverables to realign with the changed business objectives.
D. Terminate the project since it is no longer aligned with the original business objectives.
1/1 point
Correct
The project manager should "Immediately adapt the project plan and deliverables to realign with the
changed business objectives." Continually evaluating and adjusting project alignment to business
objectives and intended benefits is essential to focus on value. Adapting the project plan and
deliverables based on the changed business objectives ensures that the project remains aligned to
deliver the intended outcomes and value.
