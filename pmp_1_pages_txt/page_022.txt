96. A project manager is working with a project team that consists of members with diverse preferences and
motivators. The project manager wants to ensure that each team member is motivated in a way that aligns
with their individual preferences. Which motivation aspect is the project manager emphasizing?
O A. Intrinsic motivation
OB. Extrinsic motivation
C. Personal growth motivation
OD. Achievement motivation
Correct
The project manager is emphasizing "Intrinsic motivation," which involves tailoring motivation
methods based on individual preferences and finding pleasure in the work itself.
