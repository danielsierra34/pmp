You're leading a project aimed at improving your organization's market position. As a project manager, you
understand that delivering value is crucial. In this context, which approach aligns best with how projects
typically produce value?
O Implementing internal policies and procedures to improve organizational efficiency
B. Focusing solely on financial gains for the organization.
C. Enhancing the competitive environment by reducing legislative constraints.
OD. Creating a new product that meets the needs of customers or end users.
Correct
In the scenario of improving market position, creating a new product that addresses the needs of
customers or end users directly contributes to the value delivered. By introducing a product that
fulfills market demands, the organization not only enhances its competitive edge but also generates
revenue and customer satisfaction.

A. Designing the organization's long-term business strategy.
B. Managing the company's payroll and financial transactions.
C. Creating positive social or environmental contributions.
OD. Developing policies for legislative compliance.
Correct
In the context of a project aimed at implementing sustainable practices, creating positive social or
environmental contributions directly supports the project's objective. This involves introducing
initiatives that have a positive impact on the environment or society, which is in line with
sustainability goals.
