54. During a project audit, it is discovered that certain deliverables did not meet the specified acceptance criteria,
resulting in rework and delays. What is the main purpose of quality management processes in this context?
A. To focus on uniformity of project deliverables
B. To prevent defects and minimize rework
OC. To enhance project team morale and satisfaction
OD. To increase supply chain integration
Correct
Quality management processes aim to prevent defects and minimize rework by ensuring that
deliverables meet the specified acceptance criteria. The main goal is to deliver products and services
that align with stakeholder expectations and minimize the waste of resources.
