77. A project manager is leading a project team with members from different departments and expertise areas.
During a project meeting, a team member expresses a different perspective on how to approach a challenging
task. What action should the project manager take to align with the principles of the Team Performance
Domain?
A. Dismiss the team member's perspective to maintain consistency.
OB. Encourage the team member to conform to the majority opinion.
O C. Foster an environment that values diverse viewpoints and encourages collaboration.
OD. Reassign the task to a different team member with a similar perspective.
Correct
To align with the principles of the Team Performance Domain, the project manager should foster an
environment that values diverse viewpoints and encourages collaboration. This domain emphasizes
the importance of establishing a culture that enables diverse individuals to evolve into a high-
performing team.

members' preferences are considered.
